Bootstrap commerce catalog

Brief

To create a site-catalog for the company of a truck dealer and related products. On the bootstrap framework.

Result

The loading page has a visual definition of the site's themes and popular products. Products are categorized and visually defined by icons (images) to speed up user navigation on the site. The catalog has a filter simplifying the choice of the product. The product cards have the basic characteristics. The product page has full photos, features and a call to that action button.
